Fish Tacos

Fresh Fish Tacos for Weekday Dinner Meal

A favorite fish taco recipe! It's affordable and easy-to-make. The best part is the garlic lime crema sauce that gets drizzled on at the end.

Simple and straight-forward; you could have your children help you whip this up as well.

Fish Taco Ingredients

  • 24 small white corn tortillas
  • 1.5 lb tilapia
  • .5 tsp ground cumin
  • .5 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • .25 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter

Fish Taco Toppings

  • .5 small purple cabbage
  • 2 medium avocado, sliced
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • .5 red onion, diced
  • .5 bunch cilantro, long stems removed
  • 1 cup Cotija cheese, grated
  • 1 lime, cut into 8 wedges, to serve

Fish Taco Sauce

  • .5 cup sour cream
  • .33 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp lime juice, from 1 medium lime
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Sriracha sauce, to taste

Steps

  1. Line large baking sheet with parchment or silicone liner. In a small dish, combine seasonings: 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper, 1 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper and evenly sprinkle seasoning mix over both sides of tilapia.
  2. Lightly drizzle fish with olive oil and dot each piece with butter. Bake at 375 for 20-25 min. To brown edges, broil for 3-5 minutes at the end if desired.
  3. Combine all Taco sauce ingredients in a medium bowl and whisk until well blended.
  4. To serve the tacos, toast the corn tortillas on a large dry skillet or griddle over medium to high heat.
  5. To assemble: start with pieces of fish then add remaining ingredients finishing with a generous sprinkle of cotija cheese and finally that awesome fish sauce! Serve with a fresh lime wedge to squeeze over tacos.
