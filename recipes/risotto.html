Leek and Parmesan Risotto

A Simple and Meat-Free Meal Option

This seriously simple risotto recipe is ideal for meat-free Monday. Spring onion and leek risotto is cooked traditionally in white wine and topped with parmesan.

  • 25g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 spring onions, chopped
  • 2 leeks, trimmed and finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 150g arborio rice
  • a glass of white wine
  • 750g vegetable stock
  • 25g parmesan, finely grated

Steps

  1. Bring the stock to a simmer.
  2. Heat a large wide pan and add half the butter and the olive oil. Add the spring onions, leeks and garlic and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the rice and stir to coat then tip in the wine and bubble until reduced.
  4. Add the stock a little at a time, stirring until the rice is tender with a little bite and oozy. Stir in the parmesan and the rest of butter and season.
